Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are PreWeatherX forecasts?
PreWeatherX uses advanced meteorological models and real-time data from thousands of weather stations worldwide to provide highly accurate forecasts with up to 95% accuracy for short-term predictions and 87% accuracy for 7-day forecasts.
Can I use PreWeatherX offline?
Yes! Our mobile app allows you to download weather data for up to 10 locations for offline viewing. The data syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet, ensuring you're never without critical weather information.
Is there a free version available?
Absolutely! We offer a generous free tier with basic weather information, 3-day forecasts, and essential alerts. Premium features like extended forecasts, radar imagery, and API access are available with our paid plans starting at $9/month.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings with just two clicks, or contact our 24/7 support team. We offer prorated refunds and will help you export your data if needed.
Do you offer enterprise solutions?
Yes! We provide customizable enterprise solutions including dedicated APIs, white-label options, priority support, and on-premises deployment options. Contact our enterprise team for a tailored solution.

Still Need Help?

Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions, technical issues, or feature requests.
